Seasonal Management: Summer Peak Shaving

Objective
Reduce AC load during 2–6 PM peak hours.

Techniques
- Pre-cool home to 70°F before noon
- Use smart plugs to delay pool pumps/dryers
- Close blinds on east/west windows
- Run ceiling fans + raise thermostat 4°F (still comfortable)

Result
- 20–30% reduction in peak demand charges (SEIA, 2024)
