Smart Home Tip: Geofencing for Thermostat Control

Concept
Use smartphone GPS to automatically adjust thermostat when residents leave/return home.

Setup (Example: ecobee + IFTTT)
1. Enable geofencing in app
2. Set "Away" temp: 78°F (summer) / 62°F (winter)
3. Set "Home" temp: 72°F

Annual Savings
- 8–12% on HVAC costs (Nest Labs, 2024)

Pro Tip
Combine with occupancy sensors for multi-zone precision.
