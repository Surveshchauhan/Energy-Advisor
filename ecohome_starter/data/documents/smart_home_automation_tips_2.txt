Smart Home Tip: Automated Lighting Schedules with Motion Overrides

Strategy
Program lights to follow occupancy patterns, with manual motion sensor overrides.

Tools
- Philips Hue + Home Assistant
- Lutron Caséta + occupancy sensors

Sample Schedule
Time            | Action
----------------|-----------------------
6:00 AM         | Kitchen: 70% brightness
8:00 AM–5:00 PM | All off (if vacant)
Sunset          | Exterior: on

Savings
- Up to 35% on lighting energy (DOE Smart Home Study, 2023)
