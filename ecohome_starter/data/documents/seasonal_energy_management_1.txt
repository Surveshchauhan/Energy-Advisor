Seasonal Management: Winter Heat Retention Strategies

Goal
Minimize heat loss during cold months.

Top Actions
1. Seal windows/doors with weatherstripping (saves 10–15%)
2. Add thermal curtains (R-2 to R-4 boost)
3. Lower thermostat 1°F per 8 hrs away (7–10% savings)
4. Use ceiling fans clockwise (low speed) to push warm air down

Bonus
Programmable thermostat: drop to 60°F when asleep/away.
